üìä The 4 Growth Quadrants

Your goal: Use tactics in ALL four quadrants for robust growth strategy

1. Owned Growth

You control what you say and where (pop-ups, events, podcast, checkout pages)

2. Algorithmic Growth

Tied to social media or search (algorithm-powered channels)

3. Earned Growth

Partnerships with other newsletters or brands (tap someone else's owned audience)

4. Paid Growth

Spend money to grow your list (can slingshot small list into stratosphere)

Most bullish on: EARNED & PAID growth in this new era

üí∞ Paid Growth Tactics

Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

Three big reasons to use Meta:

1. Massive audience across two networks
2. Targeting options (cities, zip codes)
3. Low cost: $1-2 typical, as low as $0.70
Two ad formats to start:
Contrast ads: Simple message + solid color/community image
Problem + Solution ads: Explain challenge, offer newsletter solution
Check Meta's Ad Library to see what others are running

‚Ä¢ Full wall on all content
‚Ä¢ Specific verticals only (sports)
‚Ä¢ Older content only (30+ days ago)

Lead Magnets

Big guide (usually PDF). Send for free if they sign up for newsletter.

Apartment Therapy + The Kitchn:
9 lead magnets = 61,000 new subscribers
How it works:
Create guide from top-performing content. Offer in exchange for email. Send PDF when they sign up.

Checkout Pages

Anywhere someone enters email, ask if they want newsletter.

Two options:
1. Checkbox on purchase page (explain value!)
2. Offer sign-up after purchase
If multiple newsletters: Use both tactics. Main newsletter on checkout, additional after purchase.
